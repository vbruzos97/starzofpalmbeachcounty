Editorial Standards

Starz of Palm Beach County

Our Commitment to Excellence

Starz of Palm Beach County is committed to maintaining the highest standards of journalistic integrity, accuracy, and professional ethics. Our editorial standards guide all content creation and publication decisions.

Accuracy and Verification

We strive to provide accurate, fact-checked information in all our reporting. Our editorial team verifies information through multiple credible sources before publication. When errors are identified, we promptly issue corrections.

Independence and Objectivity

Our editorial content is independent and objective. We maintain clear separation between news reporting and opinion content. All opinion pieces are clearly labeled as such and represent the views of the author.

Sourcing and Attribution

We attribute information to credible sources and disclose the basis for our reporting. We protect the confidentiality of sources when appropriate and follow industry best practices for source protection.

Conflict of Interest

We maintain strict policies regarding conflicts of interest. Our editorial staff discloses any personal or financial interests that could influence their reporting. We do not allow conflicts of interest to compromise our editorial independence.

Corrections and Clarifications

When we make errors, we correct them promptly and transparently. Corrections are clearly marked and published in a prominent location. We maintain a record of all corrections for reader reference.

Advertising and Sponsorships

We clearly distinguish between editorial content and advertising. All sponsored content is clearly labeled as such. Advertising does not influence our editorial decisions.

Community Feedback

We welcome feedback from our readers and community. Comments and suggestions help us improve our coverage and maintain accountability. Contact us with any questions or concerns about our editorial standards.
